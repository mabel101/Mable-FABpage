About Me

Mable Chileshe is an Innovation Manager in Training at Mukuba UniPod. With a background in Computer Science, She is passionate about leveraging innovation, technology, and design thinking to drive social impact. Beyond her role at UniPod, Mable is the Founder of AmplifyHer in STEM, a Zambian non-profit that empowers girls and young women to explore and excel in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics through mentorship, digital skills training, and college preparation programs. Her work focuses on creating inclusive pathways for marginalized youth, especially girls from rural communities, to pursue higher education and meaningful careers in STEM. Mable’s passion lies at the intersection of innovation, technology, and social good. She believes in the power of young people to solve pressing community challenges when equipped with the right skills, mindset, and opportunities. When she’s not in the makerspace, you’ll find her mentoring young changemakers, designing creative learning experiences, or developing initiatives that bridge innovation with sustainable community development.
