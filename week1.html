Week 1: Orientation & Makerspace Safety

Date: from 23 September - 28 September

Quick Summary

This week marked the beginning of our Digital Fabrication training as UniPod Managers. The focus was on orientation, safety in the FabLab, and setting up our digital documentation tools. During the orientation, we were introduced to the program and its relevance to our work as innovation managers. The key exercise was a Virtual Safety Scavenger Hunt, which trained us to spot hazards and identify exemplary safety practices in the Fab lab

Process Documentation

The main tasks for this week included:

Virtual Safety Scavenger Hunt

  • Walked through the Mukuba UniPod FabLab with a focus on safety.

I Identified 3 safety hazards in Mukuba UniPod FabLab:

  • CNC Laser Machine: risk of smoke and damage if left on or unclosed.
  • Wood Cutting Machines: sharp blades and high-speed operation pose injury risks.
  • Glass Sheets: improper handling or storage could cause cuts.

I also identified 3 exemplary safety practices:

  • Proper storage and labeling of tools and equipment.
  • Clearly marked hazardous zones.
  • Consistent use of protective equipment such as goggles, gloves, and aprons.
